From algorithmic trading to lifesaving drones...

Machine Learning @ Berkeley's industry consulting branch works with companies and nonprofits big and small to tackle challenging real world problems across all industries. We take on new projects every semester in fields ranging from computer vision to natural language processing. Our members gain a unique opportunity to develop machine learning solutions tailored towards relevant and specific industry challenges, while providing our clients with valuable operational insights.
